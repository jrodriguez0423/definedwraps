About Us

At Defined Auto Design, we're more than just a car wrap shop, we're automotive enthusiasts with a deep passion for transforming vehicles into stunning works of art. Located in the heart of Colton, California, our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of car customization through our expertise in car wrapping and window tinting. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to quality craftsmanship, we take pride in giving your vehicle a unique and head-turning look. Our journey started from a genuine love for cars, and today, we're here to make your automotive dreams a reality. Join us in the pursuit of automotive excellence, where every car becomes a canvas and every ride tells a story.

Wrap Services

At Defined Auto Design, we take pride in our exceptional vehicle wrap service. Our skilled team is passionate about transforming your vehicle into a striking statement on the road. Using only the highest quality materials, we offer a range of finishes from matte and gloss to textured and chrome. Our wraps not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of your car but also provide a layer of protection against the elements. Whether you're looking for a full vehicle wrap or custom graphics, we work meticulously to ensure every inch of your vehicle reflects your unique style. Let us wrap your ride into a masterpiece!

Window Tint

Our window tinting service at Defined Auto Design offers the perfect blend of style and functionality. Tinting your vehicle's windows not only elevates its overall look but also provides privacy and reduces glare and heat build-up inside the car. With a variety of shades and grades, our professional installers ensure precision application for a seamless finish. Our high-quality tints are designed to resist peeling and bubbling, ensuring longevity and maintaining the pristine condition of your windows. Step into our shop to add a sleek, customized touch to your vehicle while enhancing your driving experience.
